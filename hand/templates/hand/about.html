HELPING HAND

We have created a fictional website. The main idea of the website is to provide school and hotel owners a platform where they can present their school or hotels which are currently not in use for their original purpose as an temporary hospital or health care center to help the needed in this global pendemic since all the present hospitals are currently packed. The website also provides the general user to find out the basic information like if beds are available or how many doctors are working and other basic informations which could be a very important before going to the hospital directly during the time of a nation wide lockdown.
