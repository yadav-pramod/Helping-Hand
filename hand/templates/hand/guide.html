Guidelines for the local hospital:

1.The rooms of the school or hotel should be clearly well defined.

2.While adding the school or hotel the user should enter the correct information.

3.The hotel and school should be in good condition to operate the hospital.

4.If the owner of the hotel or school wants to remove the local hospital, then he/she should request to the admin.

5.After submitting all the information the data will be sent to the Admin for verification, and only after verification the information will be allowed on the website
